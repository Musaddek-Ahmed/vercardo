Vercardo: Cultivating Green Excellence

O VERCARDO VERCARDO VERCARDO

Partner with landscape experts who understand the important role public spaces play in enhancing communities. Here at Vercardo, we're not just passionate about plants; we're dedicated to elevating landscapes into thriving, verdant sanctuaries. With over 30 years of expertise under our belt and a sprawling expanse of over 5 acres, we've established ourselves as a premier destination for botanical excellence.

Strong brands. Engaged employees. Exceptional service. These are the pillars upon which Vercardo stands. As a company with operations spanning across Barbados and reaching into the Caribbean and beyond, we've cultivated a reputation for delivering unparalleled landscaping, scientifically-based horticultural, and environmental services. Our commitment to excellence has never wavered, even as our team continues to expand.

Our passion extends beyond traditional landscaping; we're aficionados of outdoor garden plants, too. From vibrant flowering plants that grace tropical landscapes to meticulously curated selections perfect for container gardens on decks and patios, we cater to the discerning tastes of our clientele. Spearheaded by Marcus Beckles, a luminary in both construction and horticulture on the island, Vercardo has evolved from a modest contract-growing operation into a renowned supplier of the largest and most diverse assortment of palms, plants, and trees in Barbados.

Despite our growth, Vercardo remains a boutique, family-styled business at heart. Founders Marcus Beckles and Rico Holder infuse every aspect of our operations with strategic vision and personalized counsel, fostering enduring relationships with clients and affiliates alike. Our story is one of continual evolution, with each passing year bringing new opportunities for growth, innovation, and service excellence.

At Vercardo, we believe that every landscape tells a story waiting to be written. With our extensive nursery and plant selection, unwavering dedication to service, and a team of passionate professionals at the helm, we're poised to pen the next chapter in the saga of botanical and landscaping brilliance.

Our Vision

At Vercardo, we envision a future where landscapes are not just spaces to inhabit but living works of art that inspire, rejuvenate, and connect us to the natural world. Our team of skilled horticulturists, landscapers, and construction experts are dedicated to realizing this vision by seamlessly blending the beauty of nature with the functionality of modern design.

Sustainability and Conservation

As stewards of the environment, Vercardo places a strong emphasis on sustainability and conservation in all our projects. With our vast nursery of mature trees, we are able to promote biodiversity, reduce carbon footprints, and create resilient landscapes that stand the test of time. By incorporating native plants, eco-friendly materials, and innovative water management techniques, we strive to minimize our environmental impact and leave a positive legacy for future generations.

Innovation and Collaboration

Vercardo thrives on innovation and collaboration, constantly seeking new ways to push the boundaries of what is possible in landscaping, horticulture, and construction. By partnering with leading architects, designers, and environmental experts, we are able to bring fresh perspectives and cutting-edge solutions to every project, ensuring that our work remains at the forefront of industry trends.

Join Us in Transforming the Landscape

At Vercardo, we invite you to join us on this journey of transformation and discovery. Whether you are looking to create a breathtaking garden sanctuary, revitalize a public space, or construct a sustainable urban oasis, we have the expertise, passion, and resources to bring your vision to life.

Contact us today to learn more about how Vercardo is reshaping the landscaping, horticulture, and construction industry one tree at a time.
